+42 attending

Join us for an intensive 3-hour workshop where you'll learn the fundamentals of User Experience design. We'll cover the design thinking process, wireframing, and rapid prototyping using Figma.

What you'll learn:

  • User research basics and persona creation
  • Information architecture and user flows
